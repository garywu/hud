KEY NARRATIVE
================================================================================

THE STORY:
Jane is not a chatbot. She's an always-present AI companion living in your 
MacBook notch — with a face, a voice, persistent memory, and local superpowers. 
She remembers what you said last week. She listens without you asking. She 
controls your apps, accesses your files, and runs deep research in the background.

MARKET GAP:
- ChatGPT/Claude: Powerful but ephemeral (forgets)
- Siri/Alexa: Always-listening but dumb
- Replika/Character.AI: Emotional presence but no capability
- Cursor/Claude Code: Deep local access but task-scoped

NO COMPETITOR COMBINES:
✓ Persistent memory (3-tier architecture)
✓ Voice interface (wake word + natural TTS)
✓ Ambient presence (always visible in notch)
✓ Local superpowers (file access, app control, system monitoring)
✓ Open source (MIT licensed, no vendor lock-in)
✓ Face (expressive avatar with personality)

BUSINESS MODEL:
- Free: MIT open-core, self-hosted, local memory only
- $25/mo Pro: Cloud sync, multi-device, premium models, voice cloning, deep research

TARGET UPVOTES: 300+ day 1, 500+ week 1, 1K+ month 1
TARGET GITHUB STARS: 200+ day 1, 1K week 1, 3K-5K month 1
TARGET PRO SIGNUPS: 20-30 day 1, 50-80 week 1, 200+ month 1
TARGET ARR: $3K-5K by month 1
