# Jane HUD Demo Video Script
## 90-Second Product Hunt Video

**Target Duration:** 90 seconds
**Pacing:** 1.5x normal speech speed (brisk but readable)
**Style:** Warm, confident, conversational (not robotic)
**Production Quality:** Professional but authentic (not overly polished)

## TECHNICAL SPECS

### Video Format
- **Resolution:** 1080p (or 4K if available)
- **Frame Rate:** 24 fps or 30 fps
- **Codec:** H.264 MP4
- **Audio:** Stereo, 48kHz, -3dB max peaks

### Audio Mix
- **Voiceover:** -12dB to -18dB (lead)
- **Jane's TTS responses:** -15dB to -20dB (slightly lower than VO)
- **SFX:** -20dB to -25dB (ambience, subtle)
- **Music/Synth bed:** -15dB (supports VO, doesn't compete)

### Color Grading
- **Sections 1-2:** Cool, desaturated → Warm, vibrant (transition)
- **Sections 3-4:** Warm, high-energy palette
- **Section 5:** Warm, peaceful, hopeful

## VOICE TALENT & PRODUCTION NOTES

### Voice Characteristics
- **Gender:** Female (aligned with Jane identity)
- **Age/Tone:** 30s-40s, warm, conversational, slightly professional
- **Accent:** Natural (US neutral recommended)
- **Energy Arc:** Empathetic (Problem) → Uplifting (Solution) → Energetic (Features) → Visionary (CTA)
- **Pacing:** 1.5x normal speech (crisp, engaging)

### Recording Notes
- Warm, intimate mic technique (close proximity, slight proximity effect)
- No robotic TTS feel; natural delivery
- Slight pauses after emotional beats for impact
- Slight upward inflection on questions/excitement moments
- Consider ElevenLabs custom voice for consistency with "Jane" brand

### Music/SFX Sourcing
- **Background Music:** Epidemic Sound or AudioJungle (uplifting synth-orchestral blend)
- **SFX Library:** Freesound.org or Splice (notification, whoosh, chime sounds)
- **Jane Avatar Animation:** Custom, built in-house or with animator
- **Screen Recordings:** QuickTime or ScreenFlow (macOS)

- Keep Jane's personality consistent (warm, competent, patient)
