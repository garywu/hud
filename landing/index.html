Superpowers

What makes Jane different from a chatbot

🧠

Persistent Memory

Remembers conversations spanning weeks or months. She knows your projects, preferences, and context.

🎤

Voice Interaction

Wake word activation. Natural voice input and expressive text-to-speech output. Hands-free when you need it.

👤

Ambient Presence

A face in your notch. Expressive avatar that responds to conversation. Always there, never intrusive.

⚡

Local Superpowers

Access your files, monitor your system, control apps. Privacy-first: data stays on your machine.

🔓

Open Source

MIT licensed. No vendor lock-in. Self-hosted option. Extend with plugins. Built by the community.

🧩

Extensible

Plugin system for custom integrations. Deep reasoning with Opus or o3 models. Async research and tasks.
